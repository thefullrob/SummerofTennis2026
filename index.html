About the projected UTR (Momentum)

The "projected UTR" is a motivational counter, not a prediction. Real UTR moves on rated match results. Each completed practice + each weights session = +0.015 projected UTR. If they hit the full plan, both kids cruise past their goals. Point: make daily consistency visible.

The Trackers — what they're for

Three drills with explicit numeric goals get in-app trackers (visible on training/clinic days in the Today tab):

  • Spot Serve Ladder — total makes out of 100 (5 zones × 10 each × 2 sides). Goal: improve week over week.
  • First-Serve % Game — average % across 4 service games. Goal: 65%+.
  • Toss Lock — tosses landing on the spot, out of 50. Goal: 45+.

Enter a number, tap Save. Last 3 entries show with ↑/↓ trend arrows. Data persists per device.

UTR math at L1 vs L5 events

To pull a 12-match avg up 1.33 points: 8–10 strong wins over 10.5+ opponents AND avoid bad losses. At L1: Charlie's job is to compete — wins move him, losses to higher-ranked don't hurt. At L5/L3: don't lose to anyone below 9.5. Different mindset per level.
